### ü©∫ 4. Health & Stability (Probes)

| Action | Command | What to Check |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Monitor Rollout** | `kubectl get deploy,rs,pods` | Look for the **Ready** column (e.g., `11/12`). |
| **Liveness Failure** | `kubectl describe pod [name]` | Look for `Killing container... failed liveness probe`. |
| **Readiness Failure** | `kubectl get pods` | Status will be `Running` but **Ready** will be `0/1`. |
| **Event Log** | `kubectl get events --sort-by=.metadata.creationTimestamp` | Diagnostic timeline of why probes are failing. |

### üí° Final Pro-Tip: The "Describe" Hierarchy

When things go wrong in these exercises, always follow this order:

1. `kubectl get pods` (Is it running?)
2. `kubectl describe pod` (Is it a probe failure or a mounting error?)
3. `kubectl logs` (Is the application crashing internally?)
